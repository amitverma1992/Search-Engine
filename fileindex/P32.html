Sports Complex

Sports play an integral role in overall development of students and due emphasis is laid on games and sports .The college campus is also equipped with a TT tables ,wooden badminton court, a cricket field, volleyball court, basketball court to facilitate the students to nurture their sports skills and promote physical health. Arrangements are made to send students to other colleges for tournaments like university tournaments for volleyball, cricket and football.
